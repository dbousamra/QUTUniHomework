What is Yeronga Kart Club?

Yeronga Kart Club has been providing friendly, exciting and competitive karting since 2002.

We host all AKA governed karting classes at a variety of south-east Queensland and northern New South Wales tracks.


What is karting?

Karting teaches these children and adults alike, both boys and girls, advanced driving skills well before they are able to obtain their road licence.


Why kart with us?

Yeronga Kart club members have a fantastic graduation record, with many ex members now competing in various higher level motorsport tiers like Formula Ford and Formula 3.

Whether you're an experienced karter or have never karted before, you'll find your members friendly, knowledgable and always willing to give you a hand.
